Privacy Policy

Welcome to MyApp, a case management platform designed for lawyers and law institutes to efficiently manage legal cases, documents, clients, and schedules.

This Privacy Policy explains how MyApp collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal and organizational data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • User Information: Name, email, phone number, designation, bar registration number (for lawyers), institute name and address.
  • Case Information: Case numbers, titles, summaries, party names and roles (e.g., plaintiff, defendant, complainant, accused), court names, hearing dates, documents, and notes.
  • Usage & Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device info, log data, session timestamps, and actions within the app (e.g., created case, uploaded document).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain the case management platform
  • Facilitate collaboration among legal professionals
  • Ensure data integrity and compliance
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Respond to user inquiries and provide support
  • Send legal updates and important notifications

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell or rent your data to third parties
  • We may share data with authorized users within your organization
  • We may use service providers (e.g., cloud hosting) bound by confidentiality
  • We may disclose data to law enforcement if legally required

4. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Role-based access control
  • Secure infrastructure (e.g., AWS S3, PostgreSQL)
  • Regular security audits and data backups

5. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account remains active
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations
  • Until deletion is requested (as per applicable laws)
  • We securely archive your data for 6 months to improve services

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct or update incorrect information
  • Request deletion or export of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@touhidrahman.me.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., AWS, Google Analytics) to process data on our behalf. These services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

MyApp is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users via the app or email. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
